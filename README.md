🕹️ About the Game


Step into the pixel world of Zelda! 🏰 Embark on an exciting adventure through dungeons, corridors, and secret passages. Face challenging enemies ⚔️ like slicers and skeletors, avoid traps 💀, and navigate through multiple levels filled with surprises. Each level is a test of skill, strategy, and timing — defeat all enemies to unlock the next stage! Collect points ⭐ as you go and try to survive as long as possible. One hit and it’s game over! 🔄 Refresh the page to restart the adventure.

🎮 How to Play

⬅️➡️⬆️⬇️ Arrow keys — move your character through the grid

⏹️ Spacebar — shoot  enemies and clear the path

Survive the enemies to reach to the next level

📥 How to Download & Play

Click the green Code button → Download ZIP

Extract the folder to your computer

Open index.html in your browser to start playing instantly

💡 Tips & Tricks

Defeat all enemies to move to the next level

Stay alert! Each enemy moves in patterns and can catch you off guard

Keep track of your score and try to beat your previous high score

If you get hit, it’s game over! Simply refresh the page to restart the game

🏆 Enjoy the Adventure
Zelda is perfect for quick, fun gaming sessions or long playthroughs as you explore each level. Can you survive all the enemies and complete the adventure? ⚔️🛡️
